Simple Salmon Recipe

Try this Simple Grilled Salmon Recipe for a healthy meal.

Grilled Salmon

A simple soy sauce and brown sugar marinade, with hints of lemon and garlic

Ingredients:

  1. 1/2 pound salmon fillets

  2. Garlic powder to taste

  3. Salt to taste

  4. 1 tablespoon and 2-1/4 teaspoons soy sauce

  5. 1 tablespoon and 2-1/4 teaspoons brown sugar

  6. 1 tablespoon and 2-1/4 teaspoons water

  7. 1 tablespoon and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil

Directions:

Step 1

Season salmon fillets with lemon pepper, garlic powder, and salt.

Step 2

In a small bowl, stir together soy sauce, brown sugar, water, and vegetable oil until sugar is dissolved. Place fish in a large resealable plastic bag with the soy sauce mixture, seal, and turn to coat. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.

Step 3

Preheat grill for medium heat.

Step 4

Lightly oil grill grate. Place salmon on the preheated grill, and discard marinade. Cook salmon for 6 to 8 minutes per side, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.
